iSard Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

iSard is a mobile application developed by Guillem Bruix ("we", "us", or "our"), based in the European Union.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the iSard app.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at:

hola@isard.app

2. Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary to provide the core functionality of the app.

2.1 Location Data

We collect your GPS location while using the app.

We may also collect location in the background when you are recording an activity.

Purpose:

  • Track routes
  • Calculate distance, pace, and activity metrics
  • Display your activity on maps

2.2 Activity Data

We store information related to your activities, including:

  • Distance
  • Duration
  • Pace
  • Route (GPS coordinates)

Purpose:

  • Provide activity tracking features
  • Allow you to view and edit your activities
  • Generate shareable cards and replay videos

2.3 Photos

The app may request access to your photo library only when you choose to:

  • Save images
  • Export content

Purpose:

  • Save generated images and videos to your device

2.4 Screen Recording

The app may request permission to record your screen when exporting replay videos.

Purpose:

  • Generate a video of your activity replay

Screen recording is:

  • Only activated with your explicit action
  • Used only during the export process
  • Not stored by us

2.5 Crash Data

We may receive anonymous crash reports via Apple.

Purpose:

  • Improve app stability and performance

2.6 Device Information

Basic device information (such as device model and OS version) may be processed by Apple as part of system services.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • On your device
  • In your personal iCloud account (via Apple services)

We do not store your data on our own servers.

This means:

  • You retain control of your data
  • Data is managed under Apple's privacy and security standards

4. Third-Party Services

We rely only on Apple services:

  • Apple iCloud → data synchronization
  • Apple Maps → map display
  • Apple Health Data → If you choose to enable Apple Health import, iSard may read workout data from Apple Health, including workout type, duration, distance, route location data, active energy, and heart rate when available. This data is used only to import your Apple Fitness / Apple Watch workouts into iSard so you can view them, design activity cards, and generate replay videos. iSard does not sell or share Apple Health data with third parties. Health data is stored locally on your device and may sync through your personal iCloud account if iCloud sync is enabled.
  • Apple system services → crash reporting

We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase, AppsFlyer)
  • Advertising networks

5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data based on:

  • Consent → for permissions (location, photos, screen recording)
  • Contractual necessity → to provide core app functionality

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data remains:

  • On your device
  • Within your Apple iCloud account

7. Data Retention

Your data is stored:

  • Until you delete it from the app
  • Or remove it from your iCloud account

You are in full control of deletion.

8. Your Rights (EU Users)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Restrict or object to processing

Since data is stored locally/iCloud:

You can manage or delete it directly from your device or iCloud settings.

9. Children's Privacy

iSard is not intended for users under 16 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app or update the date above.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy:

hola@isard.app
